## Entry Points for Contributors

**Start anywhere. Fork freely. No permission needed.**

### Story Entry Points

- Write a scene where Soledad, as an adult, is hired to ghostwrite a memoir for a client who claims to know the truth about her mother.
- Create a chapter where Soledad discovers one of her father's early, unpublished attempts to write about his own past.
- Imagine a pivotal confrontation between Soledad and a former client whose 'memoir' she helped fabricate, who now demands a sequel.
- Write a monologue from the perspective of Soledad's father, Santiago, explaining why he chose to make his daughter his apprentice.

### Technical Entry Points

- Build an interactive 'archive' website showcasing the fictional clients' objects (tape recordings, love letters, diaries) described in the novel, with audio snippets and annotated transcriptions.
- Create a narrative visualization tool that maps the geographic origins of the clients' voices (Caribbean, River Plate, Andes) against the emotional themes Soledad extracts from their stories.
